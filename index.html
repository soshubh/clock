Dot Clock — Free Online Grid Clock & Dot Matrix Watch

Online Grid Clock

Dot Clock is a grid clock that renders the current time using glowing dots on a dark matrix. Each digit is built from an LED-style dot grid, giving a retro digital clock look. Adjust dot pitch, dot size, glow spread, accent color, 12-hour or 24-hour format, and optional seconds.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Dot Clock?

Dot Clock is a free browser-based clock and grid watch that renders time as glowing dots on a dark matrix grid, similar to retro LED displays.

What is a grid clock?

A grid clock shows time using a matrix of dots. Dot Clock lights up dots on a grid to form numbers for hours, minutes, and seconds.

Is Dot Clock a grid watch?

Yes. Dot Clock works as an online grid watch and clock in your browser, showing live time on a dot matrix display.

How do I open settings?

Click the orange plus icon in the top-right corner. The settings panel slides in from the right.

Are my settings saved?

Yes. Dot pitch, size, glow, accent color, format, and seconds preference are saved in local storage.

Can I use 12-hour or 24-hour time?

Yes. Switch between 12H and 24H in the Format section of settings.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. The clock is responsive and optimized for phones and tablets.

How do I show seconds?

Open settings and turn Seconds on.

How do I reset to defaults?

Open settings and click Reset defaults.
